SINCE 1963
1963
COMPANY
Vartan Papazian employed three of his relatives, acquired the raw materials needed, and started manufacturing made-to-order jewelry displays in a small workshop. The Papazian Company was born out of a desire to create innovative, authentic, and stylish products through exceptional techniques in craftsmanship and design.
1995
EXPORTS
Vartan Papazian was invited to Cyprus to meet a jeweler who wanted to renovate his jewelry shop. In the years that followed, many business relationships and friendships were formed.
2016
REBRAND
New logo and website.
